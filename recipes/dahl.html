Dahl

Description

This is one of my most frequently cooked dishes. It is made by combining things like red lentils, tomatoes and a bunch of spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 1/2 tbsp of minced ginger
  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Cinnamon
  • 4 dl red dry lentils
  • 500 g crushed tomatoes
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • Vegetable broth
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Lime
  • Fresh coriander
  • Rice
  • Naan bread

Steps

  1. Peel onions, carrots and garlic. Cube the onions and carrots, mince the garlic. Grate the ginger.
  2. Put the onions, carrots and garlic in the frying pan and stir for a couple of minutes.
  3. Add the ginger and ground spices. Then add the lentils and stir thorughly.
  4. Add tomatoes, water, coconut milk and vegetable broth.
  5. Let it all boil under lid for 25-30 minutes, add salt and lime to taste.
